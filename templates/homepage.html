Learn the Filipino language and ethnic customs through fun challenges and engaging small group lessons. Kamusta Kids offers virtual classes for children ages 4-11.

Why Tagalog?

Research shows that speaking another language boosts memory, critical thinking, and problem solving skills. Multilingual children also show signs of enhanced creativity and mental flexibility.

It only takes 1-2 generations to lose our ethnic language. Unless we proactively teach our children the language of their ancestors they are bound to never learn it.

Why Kamusta Kids?

We know the importance that language plays in preserving Filipino heritage. Our online classes will help your child learn Tagalog in a playfully interactive, small group setting. They will also learn how to speak with a cultural context, as we uncover traditional Filipino customs.

How Learning Works

  1. Children meet their cohort and teacher on Zoom for class once a week
  2. Children will learn Tagalog through cultural lessons, fun challenges, and team building.They’ll also practice speaking to their family in Tagalog for homework during the week.
  3. After 8 classes, your child may advance to the next level!
